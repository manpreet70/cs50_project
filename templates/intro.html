Prologue: The Triune Seal

Long ago, the Order of the Echo sealed away a tyrant’s treasure deep under the cliffs. They shaped the caves with stone, water, and sound so that only the worthy could reach the vault.

The seal will only open to the one who carries three true signs: the key kept by the Wardens, the firestone once held by the commander, and the relic guarded in the oratory above the river.

Echoes in the dark repeat both truths and lies. Watch closely, listen carefully, and remember the true names. Only then can the vault be opened.
