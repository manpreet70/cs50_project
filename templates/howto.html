How to Play

Objective

Collect required items and reach the Treasure Chamber to win. Avoid trap rooms.

Commands

  • Movement: type "Move + direction" (north, south, east, west) to move
  • Lookaround: type "Look" to see the visible items in the room
  • Collect to inventory: type "Take + item_name" to collect an item. Only 1 item at a time.
  • Remove from Inventory: type "Drop + item_name" to remove an item. Only 1 item at a time.

Notes

  • Some rooms are dark. You need the torch to see there.
  • Certain exits are locked; youâ€™ll need the appropriate key.
  • Narrow passages might limit how many items you can carry through.
  • Warning: Entering a trap room will immediately end the game.
